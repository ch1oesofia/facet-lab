How It Works

🧠

The Idea

Psychology (Internal Family Systems) says your mind is composed of distinct parts — each with its own voice and domain. Pixar's Inside Out dramatized the same model: distinct agents at a shared console.

✦

The Council

Facet Lab turns each part into a Gemini Gem — a dedicated AI agent with a defined role, personality, and domain boundary. One Orchestrator coordinates them all.

🔄

The Relay

You chat with your Orchestrator, who delegates to specialist Gems. You copy structured messages between them — the thinking is theirs, the relay is yours.

Design Your Council

✦ The Orchestrator Coordinator

The Orchestrator is a coordinator, not a specialist — it never does domain work itself. It decomposes goals, delegates to your Facets, and synthesizes their reports. Think Baymax from Big Hero 6 — the calm, non-judgmental center of the operation.

✦ Your Facets

One agent per life pillar you identified.

Council Preview

Your Council Is Ready

Each card below is a Gemini Gem. Copy the instructions and download the knowledge file — then create each Gem at gemini.google.com.

Bring Them to Life

Follow these steps for each Gem card above.

1

Open Gemini

Go to gemini.google.com → click your profile → Gems → New Gem.

2

Paste Instructions

Click "Copy Instructions" on the Gem card above. Paste into the Instructions field in the Gem editor.

3

Upload Knowledge

Click "Download Knowledge" on the Gem card. In the Gem editor, click Add files → upload the downloaded .md file.

4

Save & Repeat

Click Save. Repeat for every Gem in your council.

5

Start Talking

Open your Orchestrator Gem and give it a goal. It will respond with a Delegation Block — copy it into the target Facet's Gem. Copy the Report Block back. That's the relay.

✦

Your Inner Council is live.
